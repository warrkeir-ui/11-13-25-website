Programs & Daily Schedule

We provide developmentally appropriate, play-based learning in a nurturing, Christian environment. Children explore literacy, math, science, art, music, and dramatic play every day.

  • Infants (approx. 1.5 years & under) – loving, secure care.
  • Toddlers – hands-on discovery, social & emotional growth.
  • Preschool – kindergarten readiness with fun, structured learning.
  • Before & After School (as available) – homework help & enrichment.
